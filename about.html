About Nova — Modern Technology Marketplace

Nova is a technology e-commerce website focused on selling consumer electronics such as smartphones, laptops, tablets, and accessories through a simple and structured online store.

It is designed for students, professionals, technophiles, and Gen Z users, supporting a clear and responsive shopping experience across all devices.

The platform focuses on clarity, trust, and usability to help users make informed purchasing decisions.
